# Marketing & Growth Division: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Division Lead:** CMO  
**Total Team Size:** 12-15 professionals  
**Key Focus Areas:** Brand, demand gen, growth

### Sub-Teams

#### 4.1 Marketing Team
**Team Lead:** VP of Marketing  
**Initial Size:** 8-10 professionals

**Key Roles:**
- Demand Generation Manager (2): Paid advertising, SEO, content marketing
- Content Marketing Manager (2): Blog, case studies, whitepapers, webinars
- Product Marketing Manager (2): Product positioning, messaging, launch
- Brand & Communications Manager (1): Brand strategy, PR, events
- Marketing Operations Manager (1): Marketing automation, analytics
- Community Manager (1): User community, forums, social media

#### 4.2 Growth Team
**Team Lead:** Head of Growth  
**Initial Size:** 4-5 professionals

**Key Roles:**
- Growth Engineers (2): Product growth experiments
- Growth Marketers (2): Growth experiments and optimization
- Growth Analyst (1): Growth metrics and analysis

- [ ] Growth metrics and analytics plan created
