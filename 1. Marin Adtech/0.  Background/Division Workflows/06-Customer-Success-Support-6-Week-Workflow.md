## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Customer Success Metrics
- **Onboarding Time:** < 1 week from signup to first campaign
- **Time to Value:** < 2 weeks to first successful optimization
- **NPS:** Target 50+ Net Promoter Score
- **Customer Health:** 80%+ customers in "healthy" status
- **Retention Rate:** Target > 95% retention rate
- **Expansion Rate:** Target > 20% expansion rate

### Customer Support Metrics
- **Response Time:** Target < 2 hours for critical issues, < 24 hours for standard issues
- **Resolution Time:** Target < 24 hours for critical issues, < 72 hours for standard issues
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Target > 4.5/5 customer satisfaction score
- **Support Ticket Volume:** Support ticket volume manageable
- **Knowledge Base:** Knowledge base comprehensive and up-to-date

### Customer Metrics
- **Customers Onboarded:** 15-25 customers successfully onboarded by Week 6
- **Customer Engagement:** Customer engagement metrics improving
- **Customer Feedback:** Customer feedback integrated into product development
- **Renewal Rate:** Target > 95% renewal rate
