# Operations & Infrastructure Division: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Division Lead:** COO

**Key Focus Areas:** Infrastructure, DevOps, IT

### Sub-Teams

#### 7.1 Infrastructure & DevOps Team
**Team Lead:** Head of Infrastructure  
**Initial Size:** 6-8 engineers

**Key Roles:**
- Site Reliability Engineers (3-4): Infrastructure reliability
- DevOps Engineers (2-3): CI/CD, automation
- Cloud Architects (1): Cloud architecture

**Team Lead:** Head of IT  
**Initial Size:** 2-3 professionals

**Key Roles:**
- IT Systems Administrator (1-2): Internal systems
