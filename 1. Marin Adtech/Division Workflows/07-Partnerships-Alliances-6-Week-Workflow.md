# Partnerships & Alliances Division: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Division Lead:** VP of Partnerships  
**Team Size:** 4-5 professionals  
**Key Focus Areas:** Platform and technology partnerships

### Team Roles
- **Platform Partnership Managers (2):** Google, Meta, Microsoft, Amazon partnerships
- **Technology Partnership Manager (1):** Technology integrations and partnerships
- **Agency Partnership Manager (1):** Agency relationships

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Partnership Metrics
- **Platform Partnerships:** Applications submitted and meetings scheduled
- **Technology Partners:** 2-3 technology partnerships established
- **Agency Partners:** 5-10 agency partnerships
- **Partner Program:** Partner program live and operational
- **Co-Marketing:** Co-marketing activities active

### Partnership Value Metrics
- **Partner-Generated Revenue:** Revenue generated through partnerships
- **Partner-Generated Leads:** Leads generated through partnerships
- **Partner Engagement:** Partner engagement metrics improving
- **Partner Satisfaction:** Partner satisfaction scores > 4.5/5
