# Product Management Division: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Division Lead:** CPO  
**Total Team Size:** 10-12 professionals  
**Key Focus Areas:** Roadmap, features, design

### Sub-Teams

#### 2.1 Product Management Team
**Team Lead:** VP of Product  
**Initial Size:** 6-8 product managers

**Key Roles:**
- Senior Product Managers (3-4): Core product areas (AI, Platform, Integrations)
- Product Managers (3-4): Feature areas and verticals

#### 2.2 Design Team
**Team Lead:** Head of Design  
**Initial Size:** 4-6 designers

**Key Roles:**
- Product Designers (2-3): User experience and interface design
- UX Researchers (1-2): User research and testing
- Design Systems Designer (1): Design system and component library

---

## Week 1: Foundation & Rapid Start

### Days 1-7: Leadership & Planning

#### Product Management
- [ ] Product leadership hired (VP Product, Head of Design)
- [ ] Product roadmap v1.0 created (6-week, 6-month, 12-month)
- [ ] MVP feature specifications started
- [ ] User research and customer interviews (if possible)
- [ ] Competitive analysis completed
- [ ] Product managers hired (3-4 senior PMs, 2-3 PMs)

#### Design
- [ ] Design team hired (Head of Design, 2-3 product designers, 1-2 UX researchers, 1 design systems designer)
- [ ] Design system foundation started
- [ ] Brand identity and design guidelines established
- [ ] Initial UI/UX concepts created

**Week 1 Deliverables:**
- Product leadership in place
- Product roadmap v1.0 created
- MVP feature list defined
- Design system foundation started
- Competitive analysis completed

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Product Metrics
- **MVP Completion:** 100% of MVP features delivered and tested
- **Feature Adoption:** Key features adopted by > 80% of customers
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Product satisfaction score > 4.5/5
- **Time to Value:** < 2 weeks to first successful optimization

### Design Metrics
- **Design System:** 100% of components in design system
- **User Experience:** User satisfaction score > 4.5/5
- **Design Quality:** Zero critical design issues
- **User Research:** User research conducted weekly

### Product Management Metrics
- **Roadmap Accuracy:** Roadmap features delivered on time
- **Customer Feedback:** Customer feedback integrated within 1 week
- **Cross-Functional Coordination:** Zero blockers from product management
- **Product Metrics:** All key product metrics tracked
