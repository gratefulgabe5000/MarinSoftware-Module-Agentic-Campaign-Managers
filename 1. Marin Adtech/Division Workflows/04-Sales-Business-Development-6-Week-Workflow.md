# Sales & Business Development Division: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Division Lead:** CRO  
**Total Team Size:** 15-19 professionals  
**Key Focus Areas:** Customer acquisition, partnerships

### Sub-Teams

#### 3.1 Sales Team
**Team Lead:** VP of Sales  
**Initial Size:** 12-15 sales professionals

**Key Roles:**
- Enterprise Account Executives (4-6): Large enterprise customers ($50M+ ad spend)
- Mid-Market Account Executives (4-6): Mid-market customers ($1M-$50M ad spend)
- Inside Sales Representatives (3-4): SMB and inside sales
- Sales Development Representatives (3-4): Lead qualification and outbound

#### 3.2 Business Development Team
**Team Lead:** Head of Business Development  
**Initial Size:** 3-4 professionals

**Key Roles:**
- Business Development Managers (2-3): Strategic partnerships
- Channel Partners Manager (1): Channel partner program

---

## Week 1: Foundation & Rapid Start

### Days 1-7: Leadership & Setup

#### Sales
- [ ] Sales leadership hired (VP Sales, Head of BD)
- [ ] Brand positioning and messaging defined (in coordination with marketing)
- [ ] Initial sales collateral created
- [ ] CRM system selected and configured (Salesforce)
- [ ] First 5-8 sales professionals hired (mix of Enterprise, Mid-Market, Inside Sales, SDRs)

#### Business Development
- [ ] Business Development leadership hired
- [ ] Partnership strategy defined
- [ ] Platform partnership outreach planning started

**Week 1 Deliverables:**
- Sales leadership in place
- CRM system operational
- First 5-8 sales professionals hired
- Initial sales collateral created

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Sales Metrics
- **Customers:** 15-25 paying customers by Week 6
- **Pipeline:** 80-120 active opportunities by Week 6
- **Revenue:** Initial revenue from paying customers
- **Conversion Rate:** Target > 20% pipeline conversion rate
- **Sales Cycle:** Target < 30 days average sales cycle
- **Win Rate:** Target > 30% win rate

### Business Development Metrics
- **Platform Partnerships:** Applications submitted and meetings scheduled
- **Technology Partners:** 2-3 technology partnerships established
- **Agency Partners:** 5-10 agency partnerships
- **Partner Program:** Partner program live and operational

### Sales Team Metrics
- **Team Size:** 30-48 sales professionals by Week 6
- **Activity:** Sales team meeting activity targets
- **Quota Attainment:** Target > 80% quota attainment
- **Pipeline Health:** Healthy pipeline coverage (> 3x quota)
