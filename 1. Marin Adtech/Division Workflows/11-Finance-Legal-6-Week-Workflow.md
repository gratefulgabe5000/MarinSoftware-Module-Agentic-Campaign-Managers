# Finance & Legal Division: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Division Lead:** CFO & General Counsel  
**Total Team Size:** 6-8 professionals  
**Key Focus Areas:** Finance, legal, compliance

### Sub-Teams

#### 10.1 Finance Team
**Team Lead:** CFO  
**Initial Size:** 4-5 professionals

**Key Roles:**
- Financial Analyst (2): FP&A, modeling
- Controller (1): Accounting and reporting
- Financial Operations (1): Billing, collections

#### 10.2 Legal Team
**Team Lead:** General Counsel  
**Initial Size:** 2-3 professionals

**Key Roles:**
- Attorneys (2): Legal strategy, contracts, compliance
- Paralegal (1): Contract management, administrative

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Finance Metrics
- **Financial Performance:** Financial performance tracking accurate and timely
- **Unit Economics:** Unit economics positive or on track (CAC payback < 12 months, LTV:CAC > 3:1)
- **Budget Management:** Budget allocated efficiently
- **Financial Controls:** Financial controls and compliance maintained
- **Billing & Collections:** Billing and collections processes efficient

### Legal Metrics
- **Legal Compliance:** 100% legal compliance
- **Contract Processing:** Contracts processed within 24-48 hours
- **Legal Risk:** Zero critical legal risks
- **Intellectual Property:** Intellectual property protected
- **Regulatory Compliance:** 100% regulatory compliance

### Financial Metrics
- **Revenue:** Initial revenue from paying customers
- **Cost Management:** Costs managed within budget
- **Financial Forecasting:** Financial forecasting accurate
- **Cash Flow:** Cash flow positive or on track
