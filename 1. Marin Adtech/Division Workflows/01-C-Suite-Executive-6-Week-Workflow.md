# C-Suite & Executive Leadership: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Team Lead:** CEO  
**Team Size:** 10 executives  
**Key Focus Areas:** Strategic leadership, fundraising, major relationships

### Executive Roles
- **CEO:** Overall strategy and vision, board relations, fundraising, major customer and partner relationships, public relations
- **CTO:** Technology vision and architecture, engineering team leadership, AI/ML strategy, platform scalability and security
- **CPO:** Product vision and roadmap, product-market fit strategy, cross-functional product coordination, customer experience
- **CRO:** Sales strategy and execution, revenue targets and pipeline, sales team leadership, pricing strategy
- **CMO:** Brand strategy and positioning, demand generation, content and thought leadership, market analysis and competitive intelligence
- **COO:** Operations excellence, process optimization, vendor and partner management, internal systems and tools
- **CFO:** Financial planning and analysis, fundraising and investor relations, pricing and financial modeling, risk management
- **CPO/HR:** Talent acquisition and hiring, culture and employee experience, compensation and benefits, organizational development
- **CISO:** Security architecture and compliance, SOC 2/ISO 27001 certification, privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA), risk assessment
- **General Counsel:** Legal strategy and compliance, contract negotiations, intellectual property, regulatory compliance

---

## Week 1: Foundation & Rapid Start

### Days 1-3: Executive Onboarding & Setup

- [ ] **CEO:** C-Suite hiring and onboarding coordination (CEO, CTO, CPO, CRO, CFO, CMO, CISO, GC, CPO/HR)
- [ ] **CEO:** Board formation and governance structure established
- [ ] **CEO & CFO:** Initial funding secured or committed
- [ ] **CEO & CMO:** Company branding and identity finalized
- [ ] **CEO & GC:** Legal entity and corporate structure established
- [ ] **CTO:** Technology vision and architecture defined
- [ ] **CPO:** Product vision and initial roadmap outlined
- [ ] **CRO:** Sales strategy and initial revenue targets defined
- [ ] **CMO:** Brand positioning and messaging framework created
- [ ] **CFO:** Financial planning structure and initial budget established
- [ ] **CISO:** Security architecture and compliance strategy defined
- [ ] **GC:** Legal framework and compliance requirements identified

### Days 4-7: Strategic Foundation

- [ ] **CEO:** Strategic priorities and company vision communicated to all teams
- [ ] **CTO:** Technology stack decisions finalized and communicated
- [ ] **CPO:** Product roadmap v1.0 created (6-week, 6-month, 12-month)
- [ ] **CRO:** Pricing strategy and model finalized
- [ ] **CMO:** Brand positioning and messaging finalized
- [ ] **CFO:** Financial systems and processes established
- [ ] **CISO:** Security architecture designed and implementation plan created
- [ ] **GC:** Legal framework established, initial contracts drafted
- [ ] **CPO/HR:** Hiring plan and organizational structure finalized

**Week 1 Deliverables:**
- All C-Suite executives in place and onboarded
- Strategic priorities and vision communicated
- Financial and legal foundation established
- Brand identity and messaging finalized

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Strategic Metrics
- **Vision Alignment:** All teams aligned with company vision and priorities
- **Decision Speed:** Average decision time < 24 hours for critical decisions
- **Team Satisfaction:** Executive team satisfaction and alignment

### Financial Metrics
- **Funding:** Initial funding secured and committed
- **Budget:** Budget allocation and financial controls operational
- **Unit Economics:** Positive or on track to positive unit economics

### Operational Metrics
- **Processes:** All critical processes established and operational
- **Compliance:** Security and compliance framework operational
- **Legal:** Legal framework complete, contracts standardized
