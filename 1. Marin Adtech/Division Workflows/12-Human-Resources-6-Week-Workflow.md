# Human Resources Division: 6-Week Workflow

## Team Overview

**Division Lead:** Chief People Officer  
**Team Size:** 4-5 professionals  
**Key Focus Areas:** Talent, culture, HR operations

### Team Roles
- **Talent Acquisition (2-3):** Recruiting and hiring
- **HR Business Partners (1-2):** Employee relations, culture
- **HR Operations (1):** Benefits, payroll, systems

---

## Week 1: Foundation & Rapid Start

### Days 1-3: Leadership & Setup

- [ ] HR leadership hired (Chief People Officer)
- [ ] HR team hired (2-3 talent acquisition, 1-2 HR business partners, 1 HR operations)
- [ ] Hiring plan and job descriptions finalized
- [ ] Recruiting systems set up (ATS, job boards)
- [ ] Employee handbook and policies started
- [ ] Benefits and compensation structure defined
- [ ] HR systems and processes selected

**Week 1 Deliverables:**
- HR leadership in place
- HR team: 4-5 people
- Hiring plan finalized
- Recruiting systems operational
- Benefits and compensation structure defined

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Hiring Metrics
- **Time to Hire:** Target < 2 weeks average time to hire
- **Hiring Success:** Target > 80% hiring success rate
- **Candidate Quality:** Candidate quality scores improving
- **Hiring Cost:** Hiring costs within budget
- **Team Size:** Total headcount: 150-200 employees by Week 6

### Employee Experience Metrics
- **Onboarding Time:** < 1 week for employee onboarding
- **Employee Satisfaction:** Target > 4.5/5 employee satisfaction score
- **Employee Engagement:** Target > 80% employee engagement
- **Retention Rate:** Target > 95% employee retention rate

### Culture & Performance Metrics
- **Culture Alignment:** 100% of employees aligned with culture and values
- **Performance Management:** Performance management process operational
- **Employee Development:** Employee development programs operational
- **Diversity & Inclusion:** Diversity and inclusion metrics tracked

### HR Operations Metrics
- **HR Systems:** HR systems operational and efficient
- **Benefits Administration:** Benefits administration efficient
- **Payroll:** Payroll processing accurate and timely
- **Compliance:** 100% HR compliance
