### Analytics Metrics
- **Data Quality:** 100% data quality and accuracy
- **Analytics Coverage:** 100% coverage of key metrics and KPIs

- **Analytics Adoption:** 100% of teams using analytics dashboards

### Business Intelligence Metrics
- **Reporting Accuracy:** 100% reporting accuracy
- **Reporting Timeliness:** Reports delivered on time
- **Reporting Coverage:** 100% coverage of business metrics
- **Reporting Adoption:** 100% adoption of business intelligence reports

- **Model Accuracy:** Predictive models meet accuracy targets

- **Competitive Analysis:** Competitive analysis completed regularly
- **Competitive Insights:** Competitive insights integrated into strategy
- **Competitive Advantage:** Competitive advantage maintained
