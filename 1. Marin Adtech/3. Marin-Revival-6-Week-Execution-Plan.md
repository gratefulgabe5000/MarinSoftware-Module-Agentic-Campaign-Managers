# Marin Software Revival: 6-Week Execution Plan to Pole Position

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the organizational structure and execution plan for Marin Software's rapid revival from bankruptcy to market leadership. The plan compresses all critical workstreams into a 6-week parallel execution timeline, designed to achieve pole position in AdTech while eclipsing platform giants like Google and Meta.

**Core Strategy:** Multi-path parallel execution with aggressive resource allocation, focusing on AI-first differentiation, privacy-first architecture, and rapid customer acquisition.
